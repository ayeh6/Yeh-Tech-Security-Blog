AVHzY USB tester acquired!

Tags: usb-c, voltage, amps, e-mark chip

I recently made an impulse amazon purchase of a AVHzY CT-3 USB tester. Initially saw it from a youtube video and at the time it was sold out because of the hype. Now it's finally in stock I acquired one and it is great! I am able to test my chargers and see which protocols they are able to fast charge with like samsung/apple fast charging, quick charge 3.0 or 2.0, and many other protocols. It even has an e-mark chip reader so if you have a usb-c cable with an e-mark chip, you can test to see what the cable is capable of in terms of speed and charging capabilities.

FlipperZero acquired!

Tags: infared, sub-ghz, nfc, hacking

I finally got a FlipperZero! It is such a neat tool to use, so far I have been using it as a universal remote for my fan and air purifier, but it can do a lot of other things.
